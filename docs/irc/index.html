IRC Chat

Chat with us on IRC:

  • Server: freenode
  • Chat room: #optimization_engine

You may access freenode online at webchat.freenode.net/.

To join our room, choose your username and join #optimization_engine (or use the command /join #optimization_engine).

You may find instructions to join freenode using an IRC client here.

On Linux and Windows you can use HexChat.
